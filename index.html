PRE-PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

In order to film in any of the University of Greenwich campuses you need to:

•Agree on a location, time and date for filming. • Consider equipment required and filming process • Submit RAMS for approval by your Course Tutor.

For your RAMS to be approved, you need to follow this checklist:

Are you filming in Faculty Areas or offsite? 

YES -> Obtain approval from Faculty or site owner

Are you filming in the UoG general areas (corridors, lobby, lift, etc.)?  

YES -> Submit to fmhelpdesk@gre.ac.uk for secondary approval specifying details (e.g. room number, fire route, power and lighting requirements,  date & hours of filming).

Are you filming in Greenwich Foundation Areas?

YES -> Please contact the Greenwich Foundation to submit a request on facilities@ornc.org specifying details (e.g. location, fire route, power and lighting requirements, date & hours of filming).

NO -> Approval Obtained. Start Filming!
