PRE-PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

In order to film in any of the University of Greenwich campuses you need to:

1. Agree on a location, time and date for filming.
2. Consider equipment required and filming process.
3. Submit RAMS for approval by your Course Tutor.

For your RAMS to be approved, you need to follow this checklist:

• Are you filming in Faculty Areas or offsite?
yes / no

• Are you filming in the UoG general areas (corridors, lobby, lift, etc.)?
yes / no

• Are you filming in Greenwich Foundation Areas?
yes / no
